Zichen Wang

I am Zichen, a first-year Ph.D. student at the University of Michigan, advised by Prof. JJ Park. I work on the intersections of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and generative models.

Prior to that, I obtained my Bachelor degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science at Cornell University, where I worked with Prof. Steve Marschner on differentiable rendering.
