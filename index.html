Research

My research experience first started in Mathematics and Theoretical Computer Science (TCS). Envisioning to bring rigorious mathematics into real-world applications, I worked on the k-MST approximation algorithm at my first somphomore semester and symplectic geometry at my pre-junior summer. As I continue to study more advanced Geometry courses, I became interested in the applications of Geometry in Computer Science. This sparked my interest in Computer Graphics and Rendering Algorithms.
